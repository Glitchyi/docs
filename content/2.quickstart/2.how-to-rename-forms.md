Go to your HeyForm dashboard, choose a form and click the three dots to select <code>Rename</code>.

<img
  src="/images/rename-form-dropdown.png"
  alt="Rename a form"
  data-zoomable
/>

Once you click <code>Rename</code>, a popup will appear to rename the form.
